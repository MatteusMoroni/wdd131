About Me

My Story

Hi, I'm Matteus Moroni, a Web Designer and Front-End Developer from Brazil. After nearly 10 years in tech support, I transitioned into web design, combining creativity and usability to build clean, responsive interfaces.

I’ve worked on landing pages, business sites, and UX-focused screens using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Figma. I'm also passionate about self-growth, psychology, and design with purpose. Let's build something great together!

I have experience with a range of modern web technologies. My goal is to build things that are not only beautiful and user-friendly but also performant and accessible.

  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Responsive Design
  • Version Control (Git)

My Hobbies

When I’m not designing or coding, you’ll find me diving into books on psychology, philosophy, and personal development — or getting lost in a good fantasy or suspense novel.
